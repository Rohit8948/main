OUR LEAD PASTORS

Jason & Lis Burns

Dr. Jason and Lis Burns are the founding and lead pastors of Access Church in Lakeland, Florida. In 2007, Jason and Lis started Access as a college Bible study in their living room with 15 friends. Today, thousands of people in Central Florida call Access Church their home.

Jason and Lis have three children and live in Lakeland, Florida. Jason recently completed his Doctorate in Ministry through Southeastern University, and Lis is currently pursuing a Master of Arts in ministry degree.
